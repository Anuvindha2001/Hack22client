Code of Conduct


Our hackathon is dedicated to providing a safe and comfortable environment and harassment-free experience for everyone.

● Harassment of hackathon participants is not tolerated under any circumstances. Please show the utmost respect to all other hackers. We urge that you be courteous, professional, and refrain from disrespecting or dismissing other guests. Harassment and remarks that are racist, sexist, or exclusionary are not appropriate for this event. Please contact the nearest hackathon organiser if you notice a fellow hacker being harassed at any time.
● Please show the utmost respect to our sponsors, organisers, and judges. Take some time off work to meet with them and speak with them. We kindly request that you give them some of your time if they come over to speak with you. They've come to assist you. Demonstrate how grateful you are.
● Photography is encouraged, but other participants must be given a reasonable chance to opt out from being photographed. If they object to the taking of their photograph, comply with their request. It is inappropriate to take photographs in contexts where people have a reasonable expectation of privacy (in bathrooms or where participants are sleeping).
● Any hackathon venue, including hacks, talks, presentations, or demos workshops, any parties linked with the hackathon, social media, or any other online media, is prohibited from using sexual language and imagery.



Covid Regulations

We are taking precautions against Covid 19 in order to assist and protect everyone at the venue, and everyone should be aware of the Covid protocols, which include the following;
● Maintaining social distance, wearing masks, and sanitising frequently.
● Maintain a healthy environment to reduce the risk.
● Please inform the organisers at the earliest if you are experiencing any symptoms.
● It'll be made sure that the said covid protocols are being followed.
